Background

... And why we're called Majority


For years, we read how a handful of billionaires own half the earth's assets. While billions of others subsist on under $10 - or even $2 - a day. We want to change this. Using the power of markets, not charity.


We've worked in startups all our lives. Built a few from ground up. We've seen what incredible change agents they can be.


But we couldn't find too many technology startups that were empowering the rural jobseekers, the tailors, the fishermen, the gig workers, the goatherds, the farmers, the rag pickers, the daily construction workers... you get the gist.


This may be because very little Venture Capital money goes to startups like these. Only 5% of all VC money in India in 2020 went to impact startups.


So here we are. Building a VC fund that does support startups like these.


We're called Majority, because we want to improve lives for the Majority. And we'd love to support founders and startups who have the same aims.


If you're a founder building for Bharat, do speak with us!
